CBC Senior School Pathways - Preparing for the Future

Choose your pathway to success with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum designed for Grades 10-12

🔬STEM Pathway

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics pathway focusing on innovation and technical excellence.

Core Subjects Include:

  • Mathematics/Advanced Mathematics - Building strong analytical and problem-solving foundations
  • Pure Sciences - Biology, Chemistry, Physics for deep scientific understanding
  • Applied Sciences - Computer Studies, Agriculture, Home Science for practical applications
  • Technical Studies - Drawing and Design, Aviation Technology for hands-on skills

📚Social Sciences Pathway

Humanities, business studies, languages, and social studies for future leaders and communicators.

Core Subjects Include:

  • Languages & Literature - Advanced English, Literature, Indigenous Languages
  • Humanities - History and Citizenship, Geography, Religious Education
  • Business Studies - Business Education, Economics for entrepreneurial skills
  • Communication - Kiswahili Kipevu, Kenya Sign Language for diverse expression

🎨Arts and Sports Science Pathway

Creative arts, music, theater, visual arts, and sports-related studies for artistic and athletic excellence.

Core Subjects Include:

  • Creative Arts - Fine Arts, Drawing, and Visual Design for artistic expression
  • Performing Arts - Music and Dance, Theatre and Film for stage and screen
  • Sports Science - Sports and Recreation, Physical Education for athletic development
  • Digital Arts - Media production and digital creativity for modern applications
